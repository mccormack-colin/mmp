Pricing

Please make sure to book early to guarantee a session. I advise booking your newborn session during your second trimester, but many moms book as soon as they find out they're pregnant! Newborn sessions ideally take place between 5-10 days of age and are booked based on your due date. Sessions are available for older newborns with the understanding that baby will be awake and swaddled for the majority of the session. For booking purposes, babies up to 6 weeks old will be considered newborns. After this age, babies will be booked for a shorter mileston session, without sleepy, posed portraits.

Sessions take place at my studio located in Natick, MA (just 20 minutes from Boston). I have an extensive collection of props, including maternity gowns, backdrops, blankets, bonnets, rompers, headbands and more for your little ones.
