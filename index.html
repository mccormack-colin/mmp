Mandi McCormack Photography

Mandi McCormack Photography specializes in newborn and baby portrait art. She serves the families of Boston's Metrowest suburbs including but not limited to the towns of Natick, Wellesley, Wayland, Sudbury, Framingham, Holliston and surrounding areas. She has a fully stocked studio conveniently located in Natick, MA. She will provide your family with impeccable service and create beautiful archival, artwork from your portraits. Only a limited number of sessions are commissioned per month in order to offer the maximum time for crafting your heirloom pieces. Your experience begins with a consultation to best prepare you for your session, continues with a full studio or on location session and ends with a complimentary design appointment to discuss and create pieces of art for you home which will be held and adored by your grandchildren and their grandchildren. Mandi was awarded the Beauty and Lifestyle Mommy Parents' Choice Award of 2015.
